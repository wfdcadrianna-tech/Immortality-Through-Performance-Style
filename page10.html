Emergence Movement

You form a collective of artists and citizens who refuse to conform to hologram performances. They want no part of this technology, and will not consume any media that is not created by living artists. Slowly, more and more citizens begin to realize that they are becoming tired of this hologram performance world, and begin to join the Emergence movement.
