About the Author

Hello! My name is Adrianna Farris, and I created the Immortality Through Performance narrative. This narrative was created for my Fundamentals of Interactive Design course, and it has helped me immensely in understanding the process of interactive design. I am currently attending the University of Central Florida with a major in Entertainment Management, as well as a minor in Digital Media. I sincerely hope you enjoy.
