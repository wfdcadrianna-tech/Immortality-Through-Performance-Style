Inside the Company

You accept the CEO's offer and begin work with Forever Entertainment. At first, the money and the title you hold is something to be admired, but the atmosphere of the company feels eerie to you. You soon realize their methods may be manipulative. One night while you are working late on a project, you hear a faint voice coming from beneath your office. "I never wanted this, please let me go." Creeped out and worried, you face a choice. Stick it out, or get out while you still can.
